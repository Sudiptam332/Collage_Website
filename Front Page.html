    Imperial Institute of Engineering (IIE)


    The Imperial Institute of Engineering (IIE), Kolkata is a private university located in New Town, Kolkata. It provides engineering, technological & management education. It was established in 2015 by IIE Trust, Act no. XXV and it is the third engineering institution founded by the IIE Colage Group.
